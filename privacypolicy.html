PRIVACY POLICY

We hope you will enjoy Smidgin craft gin and using the Smidgin website (our “website”) and we want to make sure your experience is as safe and useful as possible. Here you can find out how our website works, what data we collect from you, how we use it, conditions where we may disclose this data to others and how we keep it secure. By using our website you agree to be bound by the terms of this policy.

Skenderoski and Lowther DOO (“Smidgin”, “we” and “us”) takes the issue of security and data protection very seriously and strictly adheres to guidelines published in The General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), agreed upon by the European Parliament 2016/679 which is in force since the 25th May 2018. We are the data controller of any personal data that you provide us.

Any questions relating to this policy and our privacy practices should be sent to info@smidgin.mk

How we collect information from you and what information we collect
We collect personal information that you give us by filling in forms on our website or purchasing products on our website. It includes information that you provide when you become a Gin Friend and place an order on our website.

We collect the following personal information about you: Name, e-mail address, shipping address, and financial and credit card information; your IP address and clicks on our website from your use of our online services.

Why we need this information about you
We need your information and use the information we collect about you:

  • to enable us to supply you with products and services that you have requested;
  • to help us with understanding more about how our site is used;
  • to be able to send you communications that may be of interest to you, either electronically or otherwise;
  • for all other purposes consistent with the proper performance of our operations.

Marketing
It is your choice whether you receive information such as marketing from us. If you indicated that you are interested in receiving regular information about Smidgin Gin’s activities by signing up to become a Gin Friend, we will send you communications electronically. We will not contact you for marketing purposes unless you have given your prior consent. If, at any time, you no longer wish to receive this information, please send a written request to info@smidgin.mk.

Trustpilot Reviews
Skenderoski and Lowther DOO may contact you via email to invite you to review any services and/or products you received from us in order to collect your feedback and improve our services and products.
